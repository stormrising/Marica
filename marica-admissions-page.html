The School, In Brief

Marica School was founded in Tolichowki in 1991 by Marie Christine de Rochemonteix — an educator who believed that every child has a right to education, and who led the school for three decades until her passing in 2021. Her life's work was recognised with the Ordre National du Mérite from the Republic of France and the Mother Teresa Award in India.

"The purpose of education is to teach our children to think and develop a capacity to reason out facts. Children should be guided rather than forced — so that we may discover the particular touch of genius in each one of them."

That conviction still runs the school. Marica is deliberately small — 25 students per class — so that every teacher genuinely knows every child. It is a school where communication, confidence, and character are built as carefully as marks are.

Why CBSE

The board that
keeps every door
open.

Choosing a board is choosing your child's options at sixteen. CBSE — India's national board — is the one that keeps the most doors open, especially for children who may pursue science, engineering, or medicine.

Built on NCERT — the JEE & NEET foundation

India's engineering (JEE) and medical (NEET) entrance exams are based on the NCERT syllabus — the very syllabus CBSE students study every day. A CBSE child preparing for these exams is revising, not relearning. This is CBSE's single biggest structural advantage.

Strong and systematic in Maths & Science

CBSE's approach to Mathematics and Science is rigorous, well-sequenced, and application-oriented — an excellent base whether your child heads to engineering, medicine, research, or commerce.

Recognised everywhere — India and abroad

The most widely recognised Indian board — accepted by universities across India and internationally. If your family relocates, CBSE schools exist in every Indian city and across the Gulf, making transfers smooth.

Ahead on new subjects like AI

CBSE was the first Indian board to formally introduce Artificial Intelligence into the curriculum — which pairs naturally with Marica's own AI classes. Your child studies the future as a subject, not a rumour.

Balanced, not brutal

CBSE's continuous assessment structure spreads learning across the year rather than betting everything on one exam — which suits a school like ours, where children are guided rather than forced.

Academics at Marica

What the classroom
actually contains.

01
Cambridge University Press Curriculum

Internationally acclaimed curriculum by Cambridge University Press for pre-primary and primary classes (Nursery to Class V) — a world-standard foundation in the earliest, most formative years.

02
Kreedo Early Learning

Structured, activity-based Kreedo methodology in the pre-primary years — children learn through purposeful play, hands-on materials, and guided discovery rather than rote instruction.

03
Karadi Path Language Learning

The award-winning Karadi Path methodology builds English naturally — through stories, music, and immersion — the way children actually acquire language. The root of Marica's communication reputation.

04
Smart Classrooms

Every classroom is smart board equipped and e-learning enabled. Lessons combine face-to-face teaching with digital content — and online classes are available when circumstances require them.

05
Five Kinds of Labs

Dedicated laboratories for Science, Mathematics, Languages, Social Sciences, and Computers. Concepts are handled, tested, and built — not just read about.

06
AI Classes

Artificial Intelligence taught as a subject — understanding how it works, using it responsibly, and building with it. Aligned with CBSE's AI curriculum and Marica's own technology programme.

07
Google Workspace for Education

Every teacher and student works on the Google Workspace for Education platform — assignments, collaboration, and digital skills built into daily schoolwork.

08
Well-Resourced Library

A genuine reading culture, anchored by a well-stocked library and regular library periods. Readers become communicators — it has always worked this way at Marica.

09
Navneet TopSchool Parent App

Attendance, homework, notices, and exam schedules on your phone in real time. You always know what is happening with your child — no waiting for the term meeting.

🛡️ Safety, always: full CCTV monitoring across the campus, fire safety systems, and supervised dismissal — every child accounted for, every day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Asked by every family.
Answered honestly.

Is there an entrance exam?+
Nursery through Class V: no entrance exam at all. Class VI and above: a brief, informal interaction — a conversation, not a test. We want to understand your child so we can teach them well from day one.
What are the fees?+
The fee structure is shared during your visit or on direct request — it varies by stage (Pre-Primary, Primary, Secondary). There are no hidden charges, and fees are payable monthly before the 5th.
Can my child join mid-year?+
Yes, subject to seat availability. Mid-year students receive a personal transition plan, and teachers monitor the first month closely. In a class of 25, new children settle faster than parents expect.
We are returning from abroad. Can Marica handle the transition?+
Yes — we do it every year. Email us your child's report cards before you travel; we map their curriculum against CBSE, guide you through any paperwork (such as TC attestation), and prepare a transition plan so your child can start soon after you land.
My child is coming from another board. Is that a problem?+
No. Switches from ICSE, IGCSE, IB, and State Board are routine. Our coordinator maps your child's syllabus against ours and builds a bridge plan so the change feels like a step forward.
What documents do we need?+
Birth certificate, previous report card, Transfer Certificate (Class II and above), Aadhar cards for parent and child, six passport photographs, and address proof. Missing something? Tell us — paperwork should never stand between a child and a classroom.
Why CBSE rather than State Board or ICSE?+
CBSE is India's national board, built on the NCERT syllabus — the same syllabus JEE and NEET are based on, which matters enormously if your child may pursue engineering or medicine. It is portable across India and recognised internationally. See the "Why CBSE" section above for the full picture.
Is the campus safe?+
Full CCTV monitoring across campus, fire safety systems, controlled entry and exit, and supervised dismissal — every child is accounted for before leaving the gate.
How will I know what's happening with my child?+
The Navneet TopSchool parent app gives you attendance, homework, notices, and exam schedules in real time. Add report cards, parent-teacher meetings each term, and a school that calls you when something needs attention — you are never left wondering.
What is the student-teacher ratio?+
25 students per class — held deliberately for 35 years, because individual attention is the whole point of Marica. It also means entry classes do fill; if you are serious, schedule your visit early.
Does Marica have other campuses?+
Yes — we also run a campus at Kesaram, Chevella (Ranga Reddy district), which follows the SSC Telangana State Board curriculum. This page is about the Tolichowki CBSE campus; ask us about Chevella if that location suits you better: +91 99127 37288.
What is the Mandatory Disclosure?+
CBSE requires all affiliated schools to publish key information publicly — staff, infrastructure, results, and compliance data. Marica's is published in full at maricaschools.org/mandatory-public-disclosure.
